Food Finders

Food finders... Uber for food donations. We are planning on making a proof of concept web app that helps connect Non-profit food finder programs/ freelnace volunteers with hotels, restaurants, and grocery stores that have excess food. An institution that has excess food can logon to their profile and list what food they have and the quantity, this will then ping the volunteer and the volunteer can decide what jobs they intend on filling. The web app will also point the volunteer to the closest non-profit food bank. We will implement the google maps api to get the location data on the volunteers and location of food waste and a firebase database to help notify the volunteers. We will get the directions and time until arrival from google maps and we intend on using the yelp api to help auto create profiles for institutions.
